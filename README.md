## ◼️ About

**Scripture 365** is designed to guide believers through the complete Bible in a structured, manageable way. With daily readings of 3 Old Testament chapters and 1 New Testament chapter, you'll experience both the foundation and fulfillment of God's word throughout the year.

### ◾ Features

**⬛ Complete Reading Plan**
* All 365 days pre-configured with specific readings.
* Covers all 1,189 OT chapters and 260 NT chapters.
* Chronological Bible reading plan.

**⬜ Progress Tracking**
* Visual progress bar showing overall completion percentage.
* Separate OT and NT progress indicators.
* Streak counter for consecutive days read.
* Reading history to view past readings.

**⬛ Daily Management**
* Mark readings as complete.
* Track your current streak.
* View today's specific readings.
* Share your progress with others.

**⬜ Responsive Design**
* Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
* Strict, beautiful black and white aesthetic.
* Smooth animations and interactions.
* Optimized for all screen sizes.

**⬛ Local Storage**
* All progress saved locally in your browser.
* No account required.
* Data persists across sessions.
